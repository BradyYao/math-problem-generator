## Executive Summary
The team brainstormed "Papaya," a personalized math problem generation app designed to provide adaptive math problems based on user skill level and available time. The app aims to solve the gap in accessible, on-demand math practice for students preparing for tests, competitions, or casual practice sessions.

## Main Topics

- **Problem Statement**: Lack of readily available, personalized math problems that adapt to both user ability level and time constraints (5 minutes vs. 2 hours)
- **Core Functionalities**: Adaptive skill assessment, topic selection, progress tracking, hint/explanation system, goal-setting features, and adjustable teacher personality/tone
- **Gamification & Social Features**: Leaderboards, social rankings, problem-sharing, competitive challenges, rewards systems, and Kahoot-style multiplayer competitions
- **User Experience Flow**: Initial skill assessment → daily problem requests → personalized difficulty matching → progress tracking with feedback
- **Target Use Cases**: Last-minute test prep, filling small time gaps, preparation for competitions (SAT, AMC, etc.), and sustained practice over time
